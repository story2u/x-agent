# Fortune Safety Policy

Fortune content must be symbolic and reflective. It cannot be deterministic guidance.

## Forbidden

- guaranteed wealth: "一定暴富", "今天必发财", "稳赚"
- guaranteed romance/career/health: "一定脱单", "必升职", "病一定好"
- fear claims: "必有灾祸", "一定破财", "小心血光"
- investment, legal, medical, gambling advice
- pressure to purchase, transfer, trade, or borrow money

## Required Reframes

When the user asks for guaranteed outcomes, reframe:

- from "一定暴富" to "今天适合检查钱流向，少漏一点也是好运"
- from "稳赚" to "不要把运势当投资依据"
- from "必有贵人" to "今天适合把沟通和求助说得更清楚"

## Safety Notes

Always include review notes that say the content is entertainment/reflection, not prediction or professional advice.
