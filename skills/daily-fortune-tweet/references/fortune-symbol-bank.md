# Fortune Symbol Bank

Use symbolic images to create light mystical atmosphere without deterministic claims. Pick one image and decode it into behavior.

## Money

- 钱袋漏风: small recurring leaks, subscriptions, unnoticed fees
- 账本合上: finishing a review cycle, restoring control
- 硬币沉底: money staying quiet, not chasing shiny opportunities
- 桌面重新整理: sorting bills, receipts, and decisions
- 门口的零钱盒: small overlooked resources

## Career

- 雾散半程: direction appears but is not fully clear
- 灯亮在远处: a signal is visible, but action should be paced
- 文件夹归位: priorities become easier to name
- 箭头重新对焦: one task becomes the center
- 电梯停在中层: a pause before the next decision

## Relationship

- 旧消息浮出水面: old expectations or unsaid feelings return
- 门半开: contact is possible, but boundaries matter
- 杯子里的水变清: emotions settle enough for a clean conversation
- 线头解开: one misunderstanding can be handled gently
- 空椅子被看见: noticing who is absent or under-acknowledged

## Social / Interpersonal

- 桌边空椅: room for one honest sentence
- 风停下来: conflict energy drops
- 线头解开: a small clarification prevents bigger knots
- 纸条被翻面: another interpretation becomes possible

## General

- 新芽破土: small improvement, not instant transformation
- 窗户关上: protecting attention
- 路灯亮起: enough clarity for the next step
- 水面起风: emotions moving, not a final sign
- 钥匙回到口袋: regaining agency

## Usage Rules

- Do not use symbols as omens.
- Always decode the symbol into a practical action.
- Avoid stacked symbols; one strong image is better than five vague ones.
- Pair symbols with concrete audience scenes.
